STEPS FOR DANCING

By Dolly Goyal

About

Dolly Goyal is the owner & founder of the dance school “STEPS FOR DANCING” in Houston. She teaches assorted styles of dance, from Indian Classical KATHAK to India’s biggest film industry BOLLYWOOD dance. She was a born artist who started performing at the age of 4 years. From then she has been pursuing her passion and love for dancing into her profession.
Dolly Goyal took her Kathak education from BHATKHANDE SANGEET MAHAVIDYALAYA, LUCKNOW for 8 years under the shade of her Guru Lt. Dr. Madhuker Anand, who was the disciple of Kathak Legendary Pandit Birju Maharaj.
Born in the land of film, dance and music, Dolly Goyal has dance in her blood. In the dream of spreading her love of dance to the young, youths and adults, she started Steps For Dancing.
Dolly has more than 20yrs of experience in the field of performing arts from India to Singapore and now in Houston. Her belief in pure original work, to keep authenticity in giving to the society, and that anybody can have a love for dance brings out the best in her students, whether kids or adults.
Dolly Goyal strongly believes in “Learning with Fun”. Her classes comprise of tailored syllabuses, multiple performance opportunities, competition enrolments and recognised certification all in a fun atmosphere which gives anyone all they need to grow.
Steps For Dancing is a dance school where our main objective is to build a love for dancing in anyone, to give anyone the confidence to showcase their talent, and to give you everything you need to succeed and prosper in your world of dance.
Steps For Dancing offers Bollywood, Kathak, and workout dances to different age groups at different levels. Classes are moulded to fit you best and to fulfill the desire of dance in every individual.
Steps For Dancing also trains students for internationally recognized dance certifications and gives its dancers opportunities to participate in big stage performances and dance competitions.
Quench your thirst for dance with Dolly Goyal and the community of Steps For Dancing.

Services

Kathak

Kathak is an Indian classical dance form that comes from north India. Kathak is the blend of three performing arts, dance, music, and drama. Steps for Dancing’s Kathak classes will lead you on the path to learn the skills and techniques of Lucknow Gharana. Learn pure Kathak with Steps For Dancing!

Bollywood

Originating from the largest film industry, Bollywood dance inspires billions around the world for its catchy beats, magnificent moves and freestyle form. Steps for Dancing runs Bollywood dance classes for all ages both virtual and in-person.

Choreographies

Want some top tier dancers to spice up your occasion? Want to learn a dance for an upcoming celebration? Steps for Dancing has just what you need! We can customize your song and your moves to fit any skill, age, or mood! Hire us for anything from weddings to festivals.

Performances

Each Steps for Dancing student will get a chance to perform on stage in front of live audiences and during occasions with over 100 spectators! Take your dancing passion to the next level, no matter the age or past experience. Steps for Dancing has successfully completed more than 70 performances, you could be in the next!

Competitions

Steps for Dancing competition teams train year-round and win several awards. From kids to adults, everyone has the opportunity to shine on stage with Steps for Dancing. From developing confidence to building a resume, you’ll be craving the next contest!

Park Classes

Steps for Dancing provides Bollywood Dance Workout Classes free of charge at Levy Park Houston for all ages! Bring family and friends to join from any session and improve your fitness to the beat of Bollywood.

Certifications

Steps for Dancing trains and encourages students to test for the internationally recognized Bhartiya Gandharav Mahavidyalay Kathak certification. With a former examiner and dance judge, along with a 90% passing rate of students, Steps for Dancing has all the tools needed for you to level up your dancing skills and get accredited for it.
