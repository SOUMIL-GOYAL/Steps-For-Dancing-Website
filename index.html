STEPS FOR DANCING

By Dolly Goyal

About

Trained in Kathak for eight years at Bhatkhande Sangeet Mahavidyalaya, Lucknow under Lt. Dr. Madhuker Anand, disciple of the legendary Pandit Birju Maharaj, she brings deep classical expertise along with modern Bollywood flair. With over 20 years of experience across India, Singapore, and now Houston, Dolly has turned her lifelong passion into a profession. She believes in authenticity, creativity, and that anyone—child or adult—can find joy in dance. Her teaching philosophy, “Learning with Fun”, combines structured training with performance opportunities, competitions, and recognized certifications in a supportive atmosphere.
Steps for Dancing is a school dedicated to nurturing a love for dance and building confidence in every student. Offering Bollywood, Kathak, and fitness-based dance for all age groups and skill levels, classes are tailored to individual needs while keeping the joy of movement at the center. Students receive training for internationally recognized certifications and the chance to perform on stage and in competitions. More than a school, Steps for Dancing is a community where passion meets opportunity, and dancers of all ages can grow, shine, and share in the art of dance.

Services

Kathak

Kathak is an Indian classical dance form that comes from north India. Kathak is the blend of three performing arts, dance, music, and drama. Steps for Dancing’s Kathak classes will lead you on the path to learn the skills and techniques of Lucknow Gharana. Learn pure Kathak with Steps For Dancing!

Bollywood

Originating from the largest film industry, Bollywood dance inspires billions around the world for its catchy beats, magnificent moves and freestyle form. Steps for Dancing runs Bollywood dance classes for all ages both virtual and in-person.

Choreographies

Want some top tier dancers to spice up your occasion? Want to learn a dance for an upcoming celebration? Steps for Dancing has just what you need! We can customize your song and your moves to fit any skill, age, or mood! Hire us for anything from weddings to festivals.

Performances

Each Steps for Dancing student will get a chance to perform on stage in front of live audiences and during occasions with over 100 spectators! Take your dancing passion to the next level, no matter the age or past experience. Steps for Dancing has successfully completed more than 70 performances, you could be in the next!

Competitions

Steps for Dancing competition teams train year-round and win several awards. From kids to adults, everyone has the opportunity to shine on stage with Steps for Dancing. From developing confidence to building a resume, you’ll be craving the next contest!

Park Classes

Steps for Dancing provides Bollywood Dance Workout Classes free of charge at Levy Park Houston for all ages! Bring family and friends to join from any session and improve your fitness to the beat of Bollywood.

Certifications

Steps for Dancing trains and encourages students to test for the internationally recognized Bhartiya Gandharav Mahavidyalay Kathak certification. With a former examiner and dance judge, along with a 90% passing rate of students, Steps for Dancing has all the tools needed for you to level up your dancing skills and get accredited for it.
